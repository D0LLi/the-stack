Nurse projections for 2030
Learn about the 2030 projections for the number of nurses working and needed to work by state in the United States. The gradient represents if there is projected to be a deficit or surplus of nurses by 2030. Hover over a state to see the difference in the number of nurses working and needed to work for that state.
Sources: National Nurses United, U.S. Department of Health and Human Services.
